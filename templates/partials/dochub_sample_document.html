Overview

The 8500 Series Secure Routers deliver secure networking simplified. Powered by the third-generation Quantum Flow Processor (QFP) ASIC for high throughput and scale, these routers deliver robust, platform-level security, advanced performance engineering via routing and SD-WAN, and on-premises, infrastructure-as-code, or cloud management flexibility.

Each class of secure routers is designed to deliver risk reduction, enhanced reliability, and future readiness. The platform supports multi-layer defense strategies and integrates natively with enterprise security ecosystems.

Key differentiator
Purpose-built for data center and colocation environments, offering massive scalability and granular traffic optimization. With support for high-throughput workloads, physical guardrails, and advanced automation, the 8500 Series keeps infrastructure fast, secure, and future ready.

Use Cases

Deployment scenarios
Ideal for large enterprises, cloud providers, financial institutions, and national operators requiring carrier-grade reliability.

The 8500 Series is engineered for mission-critical deployments across diverse enterprise environments:

  • Secure WAN aggregation for high-performance, scalable routing
  • Data center interconnect (DCI) with high bandwidth and low latency
  • Edge services aggregation at strategic edge points
  • SD-WAN hub consolidation for centralized policy enforcement
  • Multi-cloud connectivity with consistent security policy

Security Architecture

Layered defense from hardware root of trust through application-layer inspection.

Hardware Security

  • Cryptographically signed boot chain from hardware to OS
  • Tamper-evident hardware and runtime integrity monitoring
  • HSM integration for key management

Network Security

  • MACsec encryption at line rate
  • Advanced threat detection with flow telemetry
  • Role-based access control with AAA integration
